Clash of Clans is an addictive game that combines strategic planning with fast-paced fighting. Tribal warfare builds an army of barbarians, mages, dragons, and other magical warriors. You can join a tribe where you can be promoted through military service. You can also create your own tribe and establish your royal supremacy. Getting rid of goblin thieves is only the first step to success. Your task is endless until your tribe stands out from the millions of players and dominates the world! The balance of the game is so good that players love it.
